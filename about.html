timeline

2012

The history of the first beer that refreshes and takes care of your foot started with an innovation dream from two important Canadian brands. Molson Brewery united with Gold Bond started the studies for the future of Molson Gold.

2014

LabMoGold was created and the team started to combine all technologies, studies, and flavors from the two original brands.

2018

This year we created with the flavors of Molson beer and the healing properties of Gold Bond, Molson Gold, the beer that will leave you feeling healthy and itchy-free.

2018 - 2021

Tested in top-secret situations, Molson Gold was in the big world competitions for getting the opinion of the bests athletes. Between the 2018 World Cup and 2021 Olympics, we were there, and we got excellent results. Now we have for you, healthy beer lover athlete, four delicious types of beer like Larger, Original, Porter and Pale Ale. Enjoy anytime, any season.
